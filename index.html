Profile

Samier, MA in English (TESOL) at San Francisco State University 2021, has served as an English teaching assistant in the US and abroad. He is currently a Japanese instructor at the University of Hawaii at Manoa. He received his MBA and BA in Languages from the University of New Mexico. His research interests include translanguaging and multilingualism, which provided a basis for his capstone in analyzing language policies.
